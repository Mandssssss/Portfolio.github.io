Welcome!

I’m Armando Bagason Jr, a passionate frontend developer currently studying BSCS and I love creating beautiful and functional web applications, and I’m excited to share a bit about myself with you.


My journey in web development began with a fascination for how websites are built and the creativity involved in designing user interfaces. Over time, I’ve developed a small skill set in HTML, CSS.

If you’d like to know more about my work or collaborate on a project, feel free to get in touch!
